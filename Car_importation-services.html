Kenya Car Importation Rules

  • All imported vehicles must be left-hand drive.
  • Age limit: Cars must not be older than 8 years from the year of manufacture.
  • Clearance from Kenya Revenue Authority (KRA) and payment of import duty is required.
  • Vehicles must meet Kenya Bureau of Standards (KEBS) safety regulations.
  • All necessary documents, including invoice, logbook, and original title, must be presented.
  • Personal and commercial imports may have different tax rates — check with KRA.
  • Environmental compliance: Certain emissions standards must be met for the car to be cleared.

Note: Regulations are subject to change. Always verify with the Kenya Revenue Authority before importing.
