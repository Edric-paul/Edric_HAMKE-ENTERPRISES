Frequently Asked Questions

How long does car importation take?
Importation timelines vary depending on the source country and shipping method, but most vehicles are delivered within 4–8 weeks after confirmation.

Can I track my imported vehicle?
Yes. Hamke Enterprises provides regular updates during sourcing, shipping, and customs clearance.

Do rental prices include insurance?
Yes. All rental vehicles come with basic insurance. Additional coverage options are available upon request.

What happens if a rental car breaks down?
In case of a mechanical issue, our support team will assist immediately and provide a replacement vehicle where applicable.

Can I request a specific car model?
Absolutely. You can request a specific brand, model, year, or budget, and our team will source the best available option.
